Our Story

We’re the Kollective Behind the Classrooms.

Two school founders. An award-winning CMO. A team that has lived the charter journey from petition to packed hallways.

Who We Are

Built From Inside the Charter World

KP Charter Kollective was born from a simple observation: the schools struggling most weren’t struggling because of weak academics. They were struggling because families couldn’t find them, didn’t understand them, or didn’t trust them yet.

We know what it feels like to sit in front of a state board and defend your vision. We know what it feels like to hit approval and then stare at an empty enrollment list. And we know what it takes to turn those moments into momentum.

KP Charter Kollective was built to close that gap — to be the strategic partner that founders and school leaders actually need at every stage of the journey.
