Service 01

Charter Launch & Approval

Georgia charter approval is competitive and politically nuanced. We help you navigate it strategically. From petition refinement to board interview preparation, we make sure your application is as strong as your vision.

  • Charter petition development and refinement
  • Georgia Board of Education positioning
  • Board interview preparation and coaching
  • Academic model alignment with compliance standards
  • Governance structure and board clarity
  • Business plan strengthening
Ideal for: Pre-petition founders and schools preparing for state approval

Enrollment Growth Systems

We don’t post on social and hope for the best. We build enrollment infrastructure — systems that capture, nurture, and convert families from first impression to first day. Every workflow is intentional, data-driven, and tailored to your community.

  • Pre-opening interest and waitlist campaigns
  • Intent-to-enroll funnel design
  • Open house strategy and event conversion
  • Email and SMS nurture sequences
  • Founder family momentum campaigns
  • Lottery and announcement strategy
Ideal for: Schools 6–18 months from opening and operating schools with enrollment gaps
Service 03

Brand Strategy & Identity

Your brand is the first signal families use to understand who you are. We build full institutional brand ecosystems — not just logos. Every visual and verbal choice is intentional, cohesive, and designed to grow with your school.

  • Brand identity and naming strategy
  • Color palettes and typography systems
  • Messaging frameworks and brand voice
  • School collateral and environmental branding
  • House system naming and design
  • Apparel, yard signs, and community presence tools
Ideal for: New schools pre-launch and schools ready for a full brand refresh

Messaging That Converts

STREAM, SEL, mastery learning, early college pathways — none of this matters if families don’t grasp what it means for their child. We create messaging frameworks that simplify the complex and make your value unmistakable.

  • Academic model translation for family audiences
  • Website copy and digital messaging
  • Open house and event scripts
  • Enrollment materials and welcome packets
  • Community outreach language
  • Board-level narrative development
Ideal for: Schools with strong programs that families still don’t fully understand
Service 04

Academic & Curriculum Development

We help schools design and articulate STREAM, STEAM, personalized learning, mastery-based, and early college models — and then build the language that makes those models legible and compelling to families, boards, and authorizers.

  • STREAM and STEAM model design
  • Personalized and mastery learning frameworks
  • Industry partnership framing
  • Career pathway storytelling
  • Grant alignment language
  • Academic positioning for board presentations
Ideal for: Schools with a complex academic model that needs clearer articulation

Founder & School Leader Advisory

Sometimes you just need someone who’s been in the room. We provide 1:1 strategic advisory for founders and school leaders navigating governance challenges, board dynamics, community trust, or major enrollment decisions.

  • 1:1 Charter Clarity Intensive sessions
  • Board alignment and governance strategy
  • Founder identity and communication coaching
  • Leadership transition support
  • Strategic planning and roadmapping
  • Crisis communication and stakeholder messaging
Ideal for: Founders and leaders who need direct, experienced strategic counsel
