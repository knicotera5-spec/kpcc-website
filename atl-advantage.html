ATL Advantage — On the Ground in Atlanta.

Digital visibility alone isn’t enough in competitive Atlanta metro markets. ATL Advantage puts your school in front of families where they actually live, work, and gather.

What ATL Advantage Is

Physical Presence in a Digital World

The Atlanta charter market is one of the most competitive in the Southeast. Schools that win enrollment don’t just post on social — they show up in person, in neighborhoods, and in community spaces where their future families already are.

ATL Advantage is our on-the-ground visibility program for Atlanta-area charter schools. We deploy yard signs, attend community events, build local partnerships, and create the physical presence that digital campaigns can’t replicate.

What’s Included

Six Components of ATL Advantage

Yard Sign Deployment

Founder family yard sign strategy that turns your earliest enrolled families into neighborhood advocates. Physical visibility that digital ads can’t match.

Community Event Presence

We identify and attend the community events, farmers markets, school fairs, and neighborhood gatherings where your target families show up.

Local Partnership Development

Strategic relationships with libraries, churches, community centers, and local businesses that can amplify your school’s visibility and credibility.

Open House Campaign

End-to-end open house strategy: promotion, event design, attendee journey, and post-event follow-up sequences designed to convert visitors into enrolled families.

Hyperlocal Digital Targeting

Social and search campaigns geo-targeted to the specific zip codes and neighborhoods your school is designed to serve — not metro-wide spray and pray.

Pre-Opening Buzz Campaign

A structured pre-opening campaign that builds community awareness and intent-to-enroll before a single application is submitted. We’ve driven 400+ intent-to-enrolls in 30 days.
