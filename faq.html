Results & Expectations
Results vary by engagement scope and school context. Some benchmarks from our work: 350+ family interest in 60 days, 400+ intent-to-enrolls in 30 days, 40%+ open house attendance increases, and 300+ new students added in a single engagement. We set specific goals with each client at the start of every engagement so expectations are clear and measurable.
Some tactics produce results within 30–60 days (interest campaigns, open house optimization, community events). Brand and messaging work produces results that compound over time. We build systems designed for sustained, predictable enrollment growth — not just short-term spikes.
Georgia Charter Process
Georgia is a competitive approval state. The Georgia Board of Education has high expectations for academic model clarity, governance structure, financial sustainability, and community need. Political dynamics also matter. We’ve navigated this process and know what the board looks for, what common pitfalls are, and how to position your school’s strengths most effectively.
We work with founders at any stage — from early vision development through post-opening growth. The most impactful time to engage us is either during the pre-petition phase (when we can shape how you tell your story) or immediately after approval (when the enrollment clock starts ticking). But we’ve also helped schools mid-operation that needed a strategic reset.
